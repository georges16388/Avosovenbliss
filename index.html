Our story

Baked with heart, one order at a time

Avo's Oven Bliss started in a home kitchen in Rundu's Rainbow Village, with a simple belief: every celebration deserves something made with real care. No shortcuts, no mass production — just fresh ingredients and hands that know what they're doing.

From custom cakes that mark graduations and birthdays, to cupcakes, scones and gift hampers for the people you love, everything here is baked to order, in small batches, the way homemade should taste.
